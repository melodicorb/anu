About Us

Learn more about the Anu MT team and our mission

Our Story

Anu MT is a collective of passionate developers and designers dedicated to creating open-source tools that empower users worldwide. Our mission is to make technology more accessible and user-friendly through innovative solutions.

Founded in 2024, we've grown from a small team of enthusiasts to a community-driven project with contributors from around the globe. We believe in the power of collaboration and the importance of open-source software in driving technological advancement.

Our projects span various domains including education, productivity, and creative tools - all designed with simplicity and effectiveness in mind.
